# Sales and Revenue Performance Analytics

**Executive Summary**

Using SQL and Power BI, I analyzed sales, revenue, profit, and returns data for a global cycling equipment and accessories manufacturer to track major KPIs and uncover performance insights. After examining regional performance, product-level trends, and customer behavior — including a deep-dive SQL analysis on 2020 sales data — I recommend the following actions:

1. Prioritize top-performing territories and reallocate resources from underperforming regions.

2. Focus inventory and marketing on best-selling products to maximize revenue.

3. Implement high-value customer retention strategies to drive repeat purchases.

**Business Problem**

For a global manufacturing company, managing sales performance across multiple regions, product lines, and customer segments is complex without centralized visibility. Without clear insights into revenue trends, product profitability, and return rates, it becomes difficult to make informed decisions about inventory, marketing, and regional strategy. The purpose of this project is to build an interactive BI solution that tracks key performance indicators, compares regional and product-level performance, and identifies high-value customers — enabling leadership to make faster, data-driven decisions.

**Methodology**

1. Imported and connected raw CSV files to Power BI Desktop for analysis.

2. Performed data modeling by establishing relationships across sales, product, customer, and territory tables.

3. Created calculated columns and DAX measures to derive KPIs such as total revenue, profit margin, return rate, and order volume.

4. Wrote SQL queries to analyze 2020 sales data, examining daily and monthly sales trends, top products, top customers, territory performance, and average stock-to-order lead time.

5. Built interactive Power BI dashboards to visualize KPIs, regional comparisons, product trends, and customer segmentation.

**Skills**

1. SQL: Aggregations, Grouping, Filtering, Date Functions, Joins, Trend Analysis

2. Power BI: Data Modeling, DAX Measures, Calculated Columns, KPI Creation, Interactive Dashboard Design

3. Data Analysis: KPI Tracking, Regional Performance Analysis, Product-Level Trend Analysis, Customer Segmentation

**Results and Recommendations**

The 2020 SQL analysis revealed key patterns across orders, products, customers, and territories. Territory performance analysis identified which regions generate the highest order volumes, enabling targeted resource allocation. The top 10 best-selling products by order quantity were identified, providing clear direction for inventory prioritization. The top 10 customers by order count were segmented, highlighting high-value relationships worth nurturing. Daily and monthly sales trend analysis uncovered seasonality patterns and peak order periods throughout 2020. Additionally, the average time between stocking and ordering was calculated, providing visibility into supply chain efficiency and lead time management.

I recommend the following strategic actions:

1. Concentrate sales and marketing investment in top-performing territories to maximize revenue output from high-potential regions

2. Prioritize stock availability and promotional efforts around the top 10 best-selling products to reduce stockouts and capture demand.

3. Launch a loyalty or rewards program targeting the top customers by order frequency to increase lifetime value and repeat purchase rates.

4. Use monthly sales trend insights to align inventory planning and promotional campaigns with peak demand periods.

5. Address supply chain lead time gaps by reducing the stock-to-order window, improving fulfillment speed and customer satisfaction.

These initiatives will improve regional efficiency, strengthen product performance, enhance customer retention, and support more agile, data-driven decision-making across the business.
